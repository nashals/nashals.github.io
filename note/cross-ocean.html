1 cover letter

My name is Yan Zhang. I'm from China. I'm a software engineer with years experience of distributed systems and system software development on Linux. I have built several large-scale(thousands nodes) distributed systems and worked on the infrastructure in Baidu which is the biggest search engine company in China. Then I joined a startup "Umeng" which focus mobile device and worked on data processing and analysis by leveraging Hadoop/Spark, HBase and Storm.

Besides experience of distributed systems design and implementation:

  • proficient in C/C++ and familiar with Java and Python and Scala.
  • solid knowledge of data structures and algorithms.
  • good at performance tuning and optimization of systems based on strong knowledge of CPU, Memory and IO.
  • comfortable with concepts of function programming. Read the SICP and Little Schemer at college just for fun.
  • good understanding about compiler technique. Designed a language from scratch and implemented its compiler to generate x86 assembly code.
  • learned machine learning theory and practical technique on Coursera in the past 6 months, and took part in few competitions on kaggle.

Blog: http://dirlt.com (Unfortunately all contents are in Chinese) Github: https://github.com/dirtysalt/ LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/dirlt

Best Regards Yan
